About Me

I find beauty in ordinary frames

Hi, I'm Gowtham — a freelance photographer with over eight years of experience turning fleeting moments into timeless images. My lens gravitates toward the spaces between — the unposed pause, the soft edge of afternoon light, the quiet dignity of everyday life.

My work spans portraiture, street photography, destination weddings, and commercial editorial. Clients describe working with me as calm, collaborative, and precise — I believe the best images emerge when subjects feel unseen.
